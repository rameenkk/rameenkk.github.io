Recipes

Throughout my years of baking, I would like to show my favourite recipes that I got inspiration from. highlighted.

'Banana Bread with walnuts'.

3 bananas , ripe, smashed

⅓ cup butter melted

¾ cup sugar

1 egg beaten

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 teaspoon baking soda

¼ teaspoon salt

1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

½ cup walnuts chopped

'Carrot Cake with Frosting'.

Muffins:

1 1/4 c. all-purpose flour

1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon

1/2 tsp. baking powder

1/2 tsp. baking soda

1/2 tsp. kosher salt

2 large eggs

1 c. granulated sugar

1/2 c. vegetable oil

1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

1 1/3 c. finely grated fresh carrot (about 4 large carrots)

Icing:

1 tbsp. unsalted butter, softened

2 oz. cream cheese, softened

1 1/2 c. powdered sugar

1 tbsp. milk

'Most chewiest brownies'.
